About Us

RunNUS is an annual running event organized by NUS Students’ Sports Club to promote healthy lifestyles among the NUS population and the public.

Since its inception in 2007, RunNUS has become one of the biggest and most anticipated event for NUS students, staff and alumni. Above running and fun, RunNUS will be partnering with NUS Disability Support Office (DSO) and inviting Social Service Organizations (SSOs) this year. With the tagline ‘Run For A Cause’, we aim to empower the community of People With Disabilities/Special Needs (PWDSNs) through inclusiveness.

Our Race Ambassor, Xing En, is a Year 3 undergraduate at NUS. Affected by Senior Loken Syndrome, her vision has worsened over time, causing her to be visually-impaired. Refusing to let her condition stop her love for running, Xing En will be flagging off and taking part in the 3KM Challenge at RunNUS this year.

Details

3KM (Wheelchair Friendly!)

  • Be grouped with our PWDSNs and interact with them throughout the 3km journey.
    1. Only 50 slots available so hurry!
    2. Completely free of charge
    3. Feeling unsure/worried about not being experienced enough? Fret not as:
      1. You will go through a talk conducted by Disabled People’s Association (DPA) on how to interact with our PWDSNs. More details will be given at a later date.
      2. Trained personnel will be provided by SSOs on the event day to aid in the smooth execution of the 3km challenge.
    4. Note: Before signing up, please ensure that you will be able to commit your time on the day of the talk and the event as any withdrawal will not be permitted.
  • Pair up and walk with your family/friend
    1. Sign up individually and pair up with a family member/friend on the event day.
    2. Each pair will be given a blindfold on the event day.
    3. Person A will be blindfolded while Person B guides Person A.
    4. Note: Person A and B can switch at any point in time, as the pair deems suitable. Blindfolding is NOT compulsory but strongly recommended for participants to challenge themselves.
